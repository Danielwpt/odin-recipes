Burger Recipe

Description

This burger recipe is a simple and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Chili Sauce
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices

Steps

  1. Heat the bun
  2. Cook the patty on the grill
  3. Place cheese slice on the patty
  4. Slightly cook the tomatoes
  5. Place the tomatoes on the patty
  6. After the patty is cooked, place on the bun
  7. Add on Ketchup
  8. Add on Mayo
  9. Add on Pickles and Lettuce
  10. Combine all together the bun on top of the patty
