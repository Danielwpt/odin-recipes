Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This fried rice recipe is a simple and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Pour some oil on the wok
  2. Fry the eggs
  3. Place the fried eggs on an empty plate
  4. Chop some garlic
  5. Cook the garlic
  6. Add on the rice
  7. Pour some soy sauce
  8. Add the fried eggs from just now onto the wok
  9. Serve
